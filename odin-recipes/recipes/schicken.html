Simple yet Juicy Shredded chicken

What You Will Need

  • chicken (boneless breast or theighs)
  • water or broth
  • seasonings
  • olive oil

Step by Step

  1. add chicken to pot on medium high, brown both sides
  2. season chicken heavily
  3. add enough water to touch top of meat and cover pot
  4. cover and reduce to simmer as water boils
  5. cook for 25 mins, adding water if steam leaves
  6. remove from heat, drain water, break apart meat with tongs or forks

Serving Suggestions Hot Sauce is the best
