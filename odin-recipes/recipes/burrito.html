Breakfast Burrtios to Drive With

What You Will Need

  • torillas
  • eggs
  • salsa
  • cheese
  • black beans

Step by Step

  1. scramble 8 eggs on medium high heat, stirring frequently untill almost dry
  2. add black beans, cheese, and salsa, stir for 15 seconds and cover
  3. heat torilla to increase flexibility
  4. fill middle of tortilla 1/4 - 1 /3 with egg mixture
  5. fold ends and roll
  6. heat burrito untill golden brown and cover with foil, refridgerate after cools to room temp

Serving Suggestions Hot sauce is the best
