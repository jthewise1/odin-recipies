Wise Family Kung Pao Chicken

What You Will Need

  • 1pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons white wine
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 ounce hot chile paste
  • 1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts
  • 4 ounces chopped peanuts

Step by Step

  1. Combine chicken and cornstarch in small bowl.
  2. Heat oil in large non-stick skillet or wok on medium heat.
  3. Add chicken.
  4. Stir fry 5- 7 minutes or until no longer pink in center.
  5. Remove from heat.
  6. Add onions, garlic, red pepper and ginger to skillet.
  7. Stir fry 15 seconds.
  8. Remove from heat.
  9. Combine vinegar, soy sauce and sugar in small bowl.
  10. Stir well.
  11. Add to skillet.
  12. Return chicken to skillet.
  13. Stir until chicken is well coated.
  14. Stir in nuts.
  15. Heat thoroughly, stirring occasionally.
  16. Serve over hot rice.

Serving Suggestions Hot sauce is the best.
